The Great Divide Route

Cycle the Continental Divide

The Great Divide Route is the longest cycling route in the world. It is recognized for having long dirt roads and trails that begins in Alberta and British Columbia, Canada and continues in the United States from Montana, Idaho, Wyoming, Colorado to finish in New Mexico. On our route, we will ride the journey of Montana in 20 amazing days full of adventure and great challenges.
